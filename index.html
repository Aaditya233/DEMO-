Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Chhattisgarh?

The best time to visit Chhattisgarh is from October to March when the weather is pleasant. Monsoon (July-September) is great for waterfall visits.

Are permits required for tribal areas?

Some tribal areas in Bastar require permits. We can help you obtain these permits in advance for your visit.

What are the emergency contact numbers?

General Emergency: 112
Police: 100
Ambulance: 108
